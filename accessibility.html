Our Commitment to Accessibility

Computer Systems Inc. is committed to making our website accessible to everyone. We follow the POUR principles to ensure our content is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for all users, including those with disabilities.

Web Users and Web Accessibility

Disability Description Common Barriers Accessibility Principles to Apply
Visual Includes blindness, low vision, and color blindness Images without alt text, poor color contrast, non-responsive design Perceivable: Provide text alternatives for images, ensure sufficient color contrast
Auditory Includes deafness and hard of hearing Audio without captions, lack of transcripts, no sign language options Perceivable: Provide captions for videos, transcripts for audio content
Motor Includes limited mobility, tremors, and paralysis Small click targets, keyboard inaccessibility, short time limits Operable: Make all functionality keyboard accessible, provide enough time
Cognitive Includes learning disabilities, memory impairments, and attention disorders Complex language, cluttered layouts, inconsistent navigation Understandable: Use clear language, consistent navigation, predictable design
